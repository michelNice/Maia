ABOUT ME

Bboy Fenster began his breaking journey in 2010 and has since made a name for himself in the scene. Representing the crews Gang Gangrena and Flava Beat, he stands out for his refined technique, deep musicality, and an authentic style that blends classic foundations with innovative creativity. Throughout his career, he has won numerous national championships and continues to build a strong legacy in Brazilian breaking. Born in Santa Cruz do Capibaribe, Pernambuco, Fenster has earned great respect within hip-hop culture, inspiring new generations of b-boys and b-girls with his passion and dedication to the art of dance.
